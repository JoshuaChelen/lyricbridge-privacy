LyricBridge – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

What LyricBridge does

LyricBridge is a Chrome extension that displays timed, phonetically transliterated lyrics for songs playing on YouTube Music, helping users sing along to songs in non-Latin scripts.

Data we collect

LyricBridge does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

When a song is detected on YouTube Music, the extension sends the song title and artist name to the LyricBridge backend server solely to look up and return lyrics. This data is not logged, stored against any user identity, or shared with third parties.

Data we do not collect

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No browsing history or web activity outside of YouTube Music
  • No location data
  • No payment or financial information

Third-party services

Lyrics are sourced from the LRClib API, a public lyrics database. Song title and artist name may be forwarded to LRClib as part of a lyrics lookup. LRClib's own privacy practices apply to any data they receive.

Permissions

LyricBridge requests only the permissions necessary to function. These are used exclusively to read playback state from YouTube Music and display the side panel UI. No permission is used to monitor general browsing activity.
